## AI assistance

This project is developed with **Claude** (Anthropic) as an AI pair. Claude contributes to code, documentation, and design discussions under Vincent's direction and review. All commits are authored by Vincent — AI authorship is suppressed by design via `attribution.commit: ""` in the template settings, and enforced locally by the `check_git_author` pre-commit hook.

This reflects the project's philosophy: **AI is a tool, not an author.** The human carries intent, judgment, and responsibility for every line that ships.
